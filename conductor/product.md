# Initial Concept
Personal CRM with AI-powered contact extraction and semantic search, designed as a "Networking Brain."

# Product Guide

## Initial Focus / Starting Task
The immediate starting point for this concept is to identify individuals who purchase advertisements in specific channels. The system will collect these leads and rank them using a combination of LLM analysis and heuristics.

## Target Audience
The primary users of Networking Brain are **Sales Professionals & Solopreneurs** who need an efficient way to track, rank, and manage high-value leads (such as ad buyers) across multiple communication channels without manual data entry.

## Core Value Proposition
Networking Brain acts as a **Centralized Hub**. By bringing Telegram conversations, Excel data, and other social interactions into one unified view, it eliminates silos and ensures no contact or context is lost. The AI capabilities will specifically target identifying and ranking actionable leads.

## Key Success Metrics
The product's success is measured primarily by **Search Engagement** and the quality of the ranked leads. The frequency and accuracy of semantic searches returning relevant, high-ranking contacts indicate that users are effectively leveraging their consolidated network.

## Future Vision
The most important expansion for the platform involves developing **AI Assistants**. Utilizing LLMs to draft follow-up messages or summarize complex relationship histories will transition the tool from a passive database to an active networking partner.
